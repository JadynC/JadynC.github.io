VALENTINE’S DAY COMPANIONSHIP AGREEMENT

This Valentine’s Day Companionship Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on this day, 07/02/2024, by and between Jadyn Chowdhury ("Solicitor"), and Lucy 'Baby' Helen Ryder ("Valentine"), collectively referred to as "the Parties".

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Solicitor has expressed a sincere and heartfelt desire to be on Valentine’s Day the valentine of the Valentine;

WHEREAS, the Valentine possesses an equally sincere and heartfelt willingness to entertain such a desire;

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to enter into this Agreement to memorialize their mutual understanding and promises made in consideration of Valentine's Day companionship.

AGREEMENT

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises made by the Parties hereto, the Solicitor and the Valentine (individually, each a “Party” and collectively, “the Parties”) covenant and agree as follows:

  1. Term of Agreement.

    This Agreement shall be effective on the 14th day of February, commonly known as Valentine’s Day, and shall remain in full force and effect for a period of twenty-four (24) hours.

  2. Obligations of the Solicitor.

    The Solicitor hereby agrees to:

    • Provide a gesture of affection, which may include, but is not limited to, a verbal affirmation of admiration, a card, or a tasteful gift that signifies the Solicitor’s esteem for the Valentine.
    • Conduct themselves with the utmost respect and kindness towards the Valentine.
    • Engage in mutually agreed upon Valentine’s Day activities, the nature of which shall be subject to the Valentine’s consent and enthusiasm.
  3. Obligations of the Valentine.

    The Valentine hereby agrees to:

    • Reciprocate the Solicitor's affectionate gesture in a manner befitting the day’s sentiment.
    • Provide the Solicitor with pleasant companionship, agreeing to share in the enjoyment of Valentine’s Day.
  4. Termination.

    This Agreement may be terminated by either Party upon notice to the other Party within one hundred thousand (100,000) days of the date aforementioned. Upon termination of this Agreement, the obligations of the Parties hereunder shall be null and void.

  5. Non-Exclusivity.

    This Agreement is absolutely exclusive and either Party may under no circumstances engage in similar agreements with other individuals, provided that such engagements do not occur during the term of this Agreement nor any other time thereoff.

  6. Dispute Resolution.

    The Parties agree to resolve any disputes arising under this Agreement with a spirit of goodwill and in a manner befitting the occasion.

  7. Entire Agreement.

    This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to Valentine's Day companionship and supersedes all prior understandings, both oral and written, between the Parties with respect to its subject matter.

    IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.
